Snickerdoodle Cookies

Yummy snickerdoodle cookies! Yay!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 3/4 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt

Cinnamon-sugar mixture

  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix butter and sugar in a large mixing bowl for 4-5 minutes until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla to butter and sugar mixture. Mix for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  4. Stir in flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt, until just combined.
  5. In a separate bowl, stir together sugar and cinnamon.
  6. Wrap dough and refrigerate for 20-30 minutes.
  7. Roll dough into small balls and coat in the cinammon-dugar mixture. Ensure they are completely covered.
  8. Place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, and bake for 9-11 minutes.
  9. Let cool for several minutes on baking sheet.
